FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before attending DEVCONF 2026. Find answers about registration, tickets, workshops, networking, schedules, certificates, and more.

How do I register for DEVCONF 2026?

Visit the registration page, complete the online form, and confirm your ticket. Your conference badge will be available at the registration desk on the event day.

Where will DEVCONF 2026 take place?

The conference will be hosted at the Grand Convention Center with dedicated halls for workshops, keynotes, networking, and exhibitions.

Will I receive a participation certificate?

Yes. Every registered attendee who participates in the conference will receive an official digital participation certificate after the event.

Are meals included with the ticket?

Yes. Complimentary lunch, coffee breaks, and refreshments are included for all registered attendees during the conference.

Can I attend individual workshops?

Yes. Workshops can be selected during registration, subject to seat availability.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Selected keynote sessions and technical talks will be recorded and shared with registered participants after the conference.

Is parking available?

Yes. Free parking is available at the venue for attendees throughout the event.

How can I contact the organizers?

You can reach the DEVCONF support team through the Contact page or by emailing support@devconf2026.com.
